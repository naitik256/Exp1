    About

    This app tracks your study time automatically using AI posture detection. It starts when you're writing with proper posture and pauses when distracted.

    ⚠️ Important Study Guidelines

    • 1. Keep your face and hands visible in camera frame.
    • 2. Maintain 25 cm (10 inch) distance from copy while reading.
    • 3. Keep phone/screen 40–50 cm away from your face.

    Privacy Policy

    Your camera runs only locally. No photos/videos are saved or uploaded. Your study time is stored on your device only.
